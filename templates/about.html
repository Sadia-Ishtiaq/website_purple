About NeuroScan AI

Revolutionizing brain tumor detection through artificial intelligence and deep learning

Our Mission

To provide accessible, accurate, and rapid brain tumor detection using cutting-edge deep learning technology, assisting healthcare professionals in early diagnosis and improving patient outcomes worldwide.

Our system can detect and classify four types: Glioma, Meningioma, Pituitary tumors, and normal brain tissue with high accuracy using ResNet50 architecture.

"Early detection is the key to successful treatment. Our AI system aims to make this accessible to everyone, everywhere."
